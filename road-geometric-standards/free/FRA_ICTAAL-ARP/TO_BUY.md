# FRA — Cerema road geometric-design standards to buy (no free official full PDF)

Both current full guides are sold through the Cerema boutique / gated behind a DTRF login. Only partial supplements are freely downloadable. Do NOT buy — recorded for manual follow-up.

## 1. ICTAAL — Instruction sur les conditions techniques d'aménagement des autoroutes de liaison (Édition 2015, mise à jour 2021)
- Issuer / publisher: Cerema (for DGITM), current edition (Version actualisée mai 2015, consolidée 2021).
- ISBN: 9782371800908 (2015 print), 9782371805088 (doc.cerema.fr ed.), 9782371805910 (English translation).
- Price as listed: ~37,00 € (paper; reseller snippets €32–€37). Exact boutique price not retrievable (cerema.fr returns HTTP 403 to non-browser; site appears to geoblock).
- Official store / notice URL:
  - https://www.cerema.fr/fr/centre-ressources/boutique/ictaal-instructions-conditions-techniques-amenagement
  - https://doc.cerema.fr/Default/doc/SYRACUSE/14161/ (FR notice) ; https://doc.cerema.fr/Default/doc/SYRACUSE/594138 (EN)
  - Full text also at DTRF DT6907 (https://dtrf.cerema.fr/notice.html?id=Dtrf-0006907) but the DTRF PDF viewer requires a (free) DTRF account login.
- Why paid: the doc.cerema.fr digital viewer (c-14161) returns "Indisponible / authentifiez-vous"; the full guide is a Cerema boutique publication. The freely downloadable items are only the 16 KB Légifrance instruction circular and the 4-page 2021 update sheet (saved here), not the 60-page guide.

## 2. ARP — Aménagement des routes principales : routes ordinaires, routes à trois voies affectées ou artères interurbaines (Édition 2022)
- Issuer / publisher: Cerema (for DGITM), Bron, 2022 (refonte of the 1994 SETRA ARP guide).
- ISBN: 9782371805644.
- Price as listed: sold via Cerema boutique; exact € not retrievable (cerema.fr HTTP 403 / geoblock). An older ARP page snippet showed 20,00 € but for a different/older item; treat 2022 ed. price as to-confirm on the store.
- Official store / notice URL:
  - https://www.cerema.fr/fr/centre-ressources/boutique/amenagement-routes-principales-arp-0
  - https://doc.cerema.fr/Default/doc/SYRACUSE/592036/
- Why paid: the doc.cerema.fr digital viewer (c-592036) returns "Indisponible / authentifiez-vous"; the 2022 guide is a Cerema boutique publication and is not present on the open DTRF repository.

Manual lead: a DTRF account (free registration at dtrf.cerema.fr) may unlock the full ICTAAL 2015 text (DT6907) without purchase; the ARP 2022 guide appears boutique-only.
