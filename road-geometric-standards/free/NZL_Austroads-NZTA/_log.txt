Issuer: Austroads / NZTA (Waka Kotahi)

CONTEXT
NZ adopts the Austroads Guide to Road Design (AGRD Part 3) as the primary design authority. The
current Austroads guide is login/purchase-gated (austroads.gov.au — not free, not downloaded).
NZTA (Waka Kotahi) publishes NZ-specific supplementary guidance AND hosts a free legacy manual:
the State Highway Geometric Design Manual (SHGDM, published December 2000).
